Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • User IDs – collected to store user information like 'User Balances' or 'Inventories'
  • Server IDs – collected to store servers settings where the Bot is active.
  • Server names – Accessed for bot functionality.
  • Usernames – may be read for functionality but are not stored.

No messages or message content are stored. In some cases, messages may be read temporarily for features, but they are not logged or retained.

2. How We Use Information

The collected data is used only to provide core bot functionality, such as server management, personalization, and maintaining features of Monies. Data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to secure data but cannot guarantee absolute security. Stored data is kept only as long as necessary for the Bot’s operation.

4. Your Rights

You may request removal of your data or ask questions about how your data is used by contacting us.

5. Bot Functions

The only data accessed by the bot is either stored to save user preferences or for the functionality of the bot's features.

6. Contact

If you have concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: moniesbotdiscord@gmail.com
